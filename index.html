Research

My research is at the intersection of algebraic geometry (of flag varieties), combinatorics 
(of tableaux and polyhedral complexes) and representation theory (of finite dimensional 
Lie algebras). Currently, my main interest is in various degenerations of flag varieties, 
whose study interlaces these topics and leads naturally to tropical and toric geometry.

My research is funded by the German Academic Exchange Service (DAAD) under the grant: 
"Research Grants - Doctoral Programmes in Germany."

[ Preprint ]: Symplectic PBW Tableaux and Degenerate Relations, July 2020.

Master Thesis: Toric degeneration and tropical geometry of the variety of planes 
               ( AIMS Ghana and  IMSP Benin).
