Research

My research consists of the interplay of algebraic geometry (of flag varieties), combinatorics 
(of tableaux and polyhedral complexes) and representation theory (of finite dimensional semisimple
Lie algebras). In short, I do combinatorial algebraic geometry using Lie theory.

Currently, I am working with PBW degenerations (abelianizations as a result of taking 
associated graded spaces of the PBW filtration) of the symplectic Lie algebra, in the sense of 
the associated geometry and combinatorics as said above. On the other hand, I am also studying 
tropical symplectic Grassmannians and flag varieties, and the corresponding toric degenerations.
