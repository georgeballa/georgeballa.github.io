Research

My research consists of the interplay of algebraic geometry (of flag varieties), combinatorics 
(of tableaux and polyhedral complexes) and representation theory (of finite dimensional 
Lie algebras). I am currently interested in various degenerations of flag varieties
whose study interlaces the above topics and leads naturally to tropical and toric geometry.

My research is funded by the German Academic Exchange Service (DAAD) under the grant: 
"Research Grants - Doctoral Programmes in Germany."
